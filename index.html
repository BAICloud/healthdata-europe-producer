Why Hospitals Need a Partner

EHDS Is Coming

From 2029, hospitals must catalog datasets, manage opt-outs, and share data when HDAB issues permits. Compliance is mandatory—but building it yourself is costly.

Requests Keep Growing

Researchers, AI companies, and pharma are knocking. Reviewing each request manually, coordinating with legal and IT, and tracking approvals—it adds up.

Risk and Reputation

One data breach or compliance slip can damage trust. You need audit trails, governance, and a clear process—without overloading your team.
