# HealthData Europe — Data Producer Site (Hospitals / Data Holders)

## Target Audience
**Data Producers**: Hospitals and healthcare institutions that hold clinical data and want to share it through our platform. They supply the data; we provide the infrastructure and services.

## Purpose
- **Showcase services** we offer to hospitals (not a data upload portal)
- **Clear pricing** for platform access and revenue share
- **Commercial-ready** content for sales and onboarding

## Services Offered
1. **Governance Dashboard** — Review requests, approve/reject, manage opt-outs, audit trails
2. **Dataset Cataloging** — Publish datasets for discovery
3. **Compliance & Anonymization** — GDPR, EHDS, HDAB coordination
4. **EHR Integration** — Connect existing systems via API
5. **Reporting & Analytics** — Request volume, revenue, outcomes
6. **Dedicated Support** — Onboarding, training, ongoing support

## Pricing (Reference: Integral, Vendia, Loopora)
| Tier | For | Platform Fee | Revenue Share |
|------|-----|--------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | Single-site, pilots | From €2,000/month | 70% |
| **Growth** | Multi-site, regional | From €5,000/month | 75% |
| **Enterprise** | Large systems | Custom | 80% |

*Revenue share = hospital keeps majority of fees paid by data requesters*
