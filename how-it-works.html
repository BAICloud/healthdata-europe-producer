How It Works

From onboarding to revenue—the path from hospital to platform.

1

Onboard & Catalog

We connect your EHR and systems. You publish available datasets in the catalog. Researchers can discover you.

2

Receive & Review

Data requests arrive in your dashboard. Review purpose, requester, and HDAB permit. Approve or reject with full traceability.

3

Share & Earn

Approved data flows through our secure pipeline. Anonymization applied. You share in revenue and maintain full control.
